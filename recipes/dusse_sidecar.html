Kentucky Coffee

Description

simple and elegant cocktail made with your choice of Burbon and coffee this classic provides a refreshing start to any brunch or early morning celebration for those who are unimpressed with the more traditional Mimosa or Espreso Martini.

Ingredients

  • Burbon(one ounce)
  • Honey Liqueur
  • Hot Coffee(Six ounce)
  • Whipped Cream(Optional)

Steps

  1. Get the ingredients
  2. Pre-Heat Your Mug
  3. Pour in your Burbon and Honey Liqueur
  4. Fill the rest of your mug with coffee
  5. Add Whipped Cream for garnish
