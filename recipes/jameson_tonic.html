Jameson & Tonic

Description

Jameson and Tonic is a incredibly simplistic mixed drinks/p>

Ingredients

  • Jameson Irish Whiskey
  • Tonic
  • Ice
  • Lime(Optional)
  • HighBall Galss(Optional)

Steps

  1. Add Ice to glass
  2. Add 2 ounces of Jameson to glass
  3. Add 4 to 6 ounces of Tonic water to glass
  4. Stir(Optional)
  5. Lime(Optional)
