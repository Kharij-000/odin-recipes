Moscow Mule

Description

The Moscow Mule is a refreshing tast consisting of very simple ingredients that will add fun and a touch of class to any night without fail.

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Ginger
  • Ginger Beer
  • Lime
  • Mint(garnish)
  • Copper Mug(Optional)

Steps

  1. Add all liquid ingredients into glasss
  2. Stir
  3. Add garnish
  4. Enjoy
